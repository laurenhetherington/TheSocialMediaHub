How to use Snapchat

The most common use of Snapchat is to communicate with friends via image based messages but you can also communicate by chats. Many people use Snapchat to follow celebrities. So the main use of Snapchat like the name suggests is sending messages as images. How this is done is when you open up the app you automatically are at the home/ camera screen. If you swipe to the left you are brought to the chat screen where you can choose individual friends or group chats to talk to. The difference between these chats is in a single one you have the person's name at the top whereas in a group chat you have the names at the bottom. Now if you go to the right of the home screen you’ll be brought to the discover screen where you can view your friend’s stories which appear at the top and Subscription stories which appear at the top and then below that are stories of people who you have subscribed too and below that are stories from companies and other creator that you may also like. To view a story all you have to do is tap it and it will begin to play.
